Italy is a country where beauty is in every corner. From the picturesque streets of Rome to the canals of Venice, from the centuries-old ruins of the Colosseum to the romantic beaches of the Amalfi Coast, every corner breathes history and art.

Italy is famous for its wine regions, where you can taste some of the best wines in the world, and the authentic taste of gelato. Italian people live in the rhythm of "la dolce vita" - the sweet life - and everyone who comes here falls in love with this calm and at the same time rich atmosphere.

England is a country with ancient charm, where history harmoniously intertwines with modernity. From the grandeur of London with its famous Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, and the Thames, to quiet, picturesque English villages with stone houses and blooming gardens.

A walk along the southern coast of England, a visit to Stonehenge, or a tea ceremony in typical English cafes are moments that will be remembered for a lifetime. England is a place where you can experience the tranquility of provincial villages and the energy of a modern metropolis at the same time.

Spain is a country that combines rich history, unique architecture, warm sandy beaches, and delicious Mediterranean cuisine. Each city has its own unique features, from the grand Gothic cathedrals of Barcelona and Madrid to the sunny beaches of the Costa Brava.

In Spain, you can see a colorful fiesta, taste traditional paella, dance flamenco, or simply enjoy a walk through the streets filled with the spirit of romance and history.
